Lord of the Rings

On the 111th birthday of his uncle Bilbo, the young hobbit Frodo inherits a magic
ring. But with the inheritance comes great responsibility, for the ring turns out to
be the one ring of the evil wizard Sauron, who wants to bring all of Middle-earth to ruin.
The only way to save Middle-earth is to destroy the Ring once and for all. But in order to do so,
Frodo must go to the far-off Mount Doom, and many dark dangers lurk on the way.

Die Hard

New York police officer John McClane flies to Los Angeles on Christmas Eve to
celebrate the holiday with his local family. He also hopes to reconcile with his wife
Holly. But when he goes to his wife's Christmas party, it comes to an abrupt end because
it is stormed by terrorists around Hans Gruber. Only McClane manages to escape
unnoticed in the chaos. Now he has to face the terrorists alone and fight for life and death.

Mytix

Thomas works as a programmer and runs part-time jobs as a professional hacker under the pseudonym Neo.
He has the feeling that something unimaginable and mysterious is guiding his life.
The feeling becomes a certainty when the hacker Trinity introduces him to the leader Morpheus.
Morpheus offers Neo a chance to learn a truth beyond the limits of his imagination.
Neo becomes a border crosser and is freed from his old life, the Matrix.

Cast away

FedEx employee Chuck Noland is stranded on a small, uninhabited island in the Pacific
Ocean after a plane crash. Suddenly torn from civilization, he has to come to terms
with the circumstances there. A daily struggle for survival begins. To keep
from going insane in solitude, Chuck talks to himself with a volleyball he names Wilson.
Together they wait for rescue until Chuck decides to take his fate into his own hands.
